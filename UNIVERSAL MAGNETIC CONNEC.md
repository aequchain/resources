## **PHASE 1: SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE**  

### **I. MAGNETIC CONNECTOR MODULE (MCM)**  
**Structure:**  
- **Core:** Neodymium-free, iron-nitride (Fe₁₆N₂) magnets (eco-mined, 98% recyclable).  
- **Alignment Ring:** Biodegradable PLA (polylactic acid) with embedded NFC chip for device recognition.  
- **Contact Pins:** Liquid metal alloy (GaInSn) in laser-etched biodegradable PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoate) housing.  
- **Shielding:** Mycelium-based biocomposite to prevent EMI/RFI.  

**Design Features:**  
- **Self-Aligning:** 360° rotational symmetry; ±15° angular tolerance.  
- **Breakaway Safety:** 8kg lateral force threshold (prevents trip hazards).  
- **Waterproof:** IP68 rating via self-sealing PHA gaskets.  

### **II. MAGNETIC SOCKET MODULE (MSM)**  
**Structure:**  
- **Receptor Plate:** Fe₁₆N₂ magnet array with repelling polarity for secure latching.  
- **Pin Guides:** Laser-cut bamboo fiber reinforced with spider silk protein.  
- **Energy Harvesting:** Piezoelectric crystals (lead-free) convert insertion force to trickle-charge devices.  

**Integration:**  
- Backward compatibility via biodegradable adapters (legacy Phase-Out Kit).
